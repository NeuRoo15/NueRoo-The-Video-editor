## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

NueRoo makes creating content incredibly simple. Here is how you can create your first masterpiece:

1.  **Start a New Project:** Open the app and tap the **+** button or select a starter template on the home screen.
2.  **Import Media:** Choose photos or video clips directly from your phone's gallery.
3.  **Arrange & Edit:**
    *   Drag and drop clips on the timeline to change their order.
    *   Use the **Split** tool to cut unwanted parts.
    *   Tap the **Transitions** icon between two clips to apply a beautiful GLSL animation.
4.  **Polish with Audio & Text:**
    *   Tap the **Audio** button to add music, sound effects, or record a voiceover.
    *   Add stylized text overlays using our collection of premium designer fonts.
5.  **Export & Share:** Export your final video in high definition. Once exported, you can save it directly to your photo gallery or share it instantly on **Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok, Facebook**, and more!
